📱 SMS Setup — One-Time Configuration
StudyAI uses Twilio to send real SMS notifications for free — no server needed via a CORS proxy. Follow the 3 steps below to activate.
×
1
Create a free Twilio account
Go to twilio.com/try-twilio → Sign up free (free trial credit included, no credit card required initially). Verify your phone number to activate.
2
Get a Twilio phone number
In your Twilio Console → Phone Numbers → Get a Number → choose a number with SMS capability. This becomes your sender number. Copy it in E.164 format, e.g. +12025551234.
3
Copy your credentials
In your Twilio Console Dashboard, copy your Account SID and Auth Token. These are used to authenticate your SMS requests.
